## üí∞ Pricing Notes

Most of these TLDs are under $20/year:
- `.io` - Usually $30-40/year (higher end, but popular for tech)
- `.ai` - Usually $60-100/year (premium pricing)
- `.co` - Usually $10-30/year
- `.al`, `.ie`, `.ch`, `.ly`, `.er` - Varies, usually $15-50/year
- `.works`, `.space`, `.lol` - Usually $5-25/year

**Budget-friendly options under $20:**
- Focus on `.co`, `.works`, `.space`, `.lol` extensions
- Some `.io` domains may be available under $20 on sales
- Creative hacks like `chaoti.ch`, `etern.al` can be affordable
